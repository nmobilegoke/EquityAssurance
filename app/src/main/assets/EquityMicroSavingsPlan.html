EQUITY MICRO SAVINGS PLAN

DESCRIPTIONS:

This is an investment linked plan that provides both savings and protection benefit to the policyholder. Premiums paid are all allocated to policyholder fund/account that earns a guaranteed investment return. The protection benefit provides cover on death of policyholder and it is 5 times the annual premiums subject to a maximum of one million naira (N1, 000,000).


Product Features

  • The policy term is minimum of 2 years.

  • The minimum daily premium is N50 or equivalent of N1, 500 a month.

  • 100% of premium is allocated to the policyholder account

  • The life assurance benefit is defined as 5 times annual premiums subject to a maximum of one million naira (N1, 000,000).

  • The policy offers a cash withdrawal benefit option up to 25% of the fund value after year 2.

  • The amount of premiums can be reviewed upwards during the term of the policy


PRICING:

The minimum daily premium is N50 or equivalent of N1, 500 a month.
