EQUITY TARGET INVESTMENT PLAN

DESCRIPTIONS:

This is an investment linked plan that provides both savings and protection benefit to the policyholder. 95% of Premiums paid are allocated to policyholderâ€™s fund/account that earns a guaranteed investment return. The unallocated 5% of premium is used to provide the protection benefit cover on death or permanent disability of the policyholder


Product Features

  • The policy term is a minimum of 2 years and a maximum of 10 years.

  • Minimum of N100 daily or N1,000 weekly and N5,000 monthly contribution is allowed

  • Maximum age at entry for the policy is 55 years.

  • The contribution amount could be reviewed upwards at any time during the term of the policy


Benefits Payable

Death Benefit

1 In the event of death of the policyholder before the maturity date, the guaranteed Sum Assured and the Policyholders Account Balance becomes payable.

Maturity/Surrender Benefit

2 The balance in the savings account is fully payable at maturity. The savings account is the accumulation of contributions less deductions, plus interest
