HOUSEOWNERS COMPREHENSIVE INSURANCE {LANDLORD}

Equity House owners comprehensive insurance is apply to both building and the contents of private dwelling and has the following covers; fire & special perils, burglary/theft, public liability and personal accident.

We can also provide extension in the policy to cover the following, temporary removed contents {items taken for repairs, cleaning etc.}, loss of rent due to loss of or damage to the building and also all risk cover on movable items.


There are basically five {5} sections in the policy for comprehensive coverage;

  • Building {boundary walls, fences, drainage system}

  • Contents {household goods, personal effects, fixtures, fittings}

  • Additional expenses

  • Public liability

  • Personal Accident {for death of the insured only}
