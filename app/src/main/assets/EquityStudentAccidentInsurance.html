EQUITY STUDENT ACCIDENT INSURANCE

DESCRIPTIONS:

This is a group term assurance policy designed to cover fees of children in the short term on death of their sponsors. The policy will also cover emergency medical expenses for students.

The policy will be sold to the targeted schools on a pooled basis and not to individual parents.

2. Death Benefit / Permanent Total Disability

The Death and Permanent Total Disability benefits are at two cover options illustrated below. Only one payment is made that is, there will be no payment on natural or accidental death after a TPD claim has been made.

The policy pays N200,000 under Option 1 and N500,000 under Option 2

3. Emergency Medical Expenses

The policy pays medical expenses up to N 50,000.

Maximum age

Allowed maximum insured age of sponsor will be 65 years.
