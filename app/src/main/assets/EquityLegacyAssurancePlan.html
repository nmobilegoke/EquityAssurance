EQUITY LEGACY ASSURANCE PLAN

DESCRIPTIONS:

This is an annual renewable protection policy. The policy offers a guaranteed death benefit only on death of the life insured to the estate of the policyholder.

Product Features
  • TPremium frequency is daily.
  • The maximum age at entry is 70 years.
  • The policy terminates on death and a guaranteed sum is paid to the beneficiary.
  • The policy is a yearly policy that is renewable every year.

Benefits Payable

Death Benefit

1 In the event of death the policy is terminated and a guaranteed sum is paid to the beneficiary

Maturity Benefit

2 There is no maturity benefit.

Benefit Payment Conditions

Waiting Periods

3 There is no waiting period in the event of a claim.
