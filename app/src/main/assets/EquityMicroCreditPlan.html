EQUITY MICRO CREDIT ASSURANCE

DESCRIPTIONS:

This product is a credit life insurance policy designed to repay the outstanding balance of the loan amount in the event of death or loss of job of the policyholder.


Product Features:

  • The initial sum assured under the contract will be the original loan amount, and this will reduce as repayments are made. The sum assured will then be the outstanding loan amount at the time of a claim.

  • The policy pays claim on death or loss of job of a policy holder.

  • The loss of job benefit cover is for a maximum period of six months.

  • The policy term is the same as the loan term (12 months).

  • The loan amount ranges from minimum of N50, 000.00 to maximum of N1M

Benefits Payable

Death and Loss of Job Benefit

1   A lump sum equal to the outstanding loan amount at the time of death or loss of job of policy holder becomes payable.

Surrender Value Benefit

2   The policy does not provide any cash value in the event of surrender


PRICING:

Premium Frequency

Single premiums are payable in advance and at onset of the contract.
