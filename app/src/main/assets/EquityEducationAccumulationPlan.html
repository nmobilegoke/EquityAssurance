EQUITY EDUCATION ACCUMULATION PLAN

DESCRIPTIONS: This is an education plan designed to help policyholders to save towards their children’s education. The product also provides a guaranteed benefit on death of policyholder before the maturity date. Premiums from policyholder are all allocated to a policyholder account which offers a guaranteed interest amount annually.

Product Features:

  • The policy term is a minimum of 2 years.
  • The maximum age at entry is 55 years.
  • The minimum regular premium payment of N5,000 monthly, N15,000 quarterly, N30,000 half yearly and N60,000 yearly.
  • 100% of premium is allocated to the policyholder account.
  • The policy offers a cash withdrawal benefit option up to 25% of the fund value
  • The amount of payment can be reviewed upward at any time during the term of the policy.

Benefits Payable

Death or Permanent Disability Benefit

1 In the event of death or permanent disability of sponsor before maturity date, the higher of a guaranteed sum of one million naira (N1, 000, 000) or the accumulated contribution with interest is paid into a named child’s education account

Maturity Benefit

2 The accumulated premiums plus interest are paid at maturity.

Withdrawal Benefit

3 The product allows a cash withdrawal benefit option of up to 25% from the accumulated account provided the policy has been in place for a minimum period of 2 years.

Surrender benefit

4 The accumulated premiums plus interest less surrender penalty are paid on surrender.
