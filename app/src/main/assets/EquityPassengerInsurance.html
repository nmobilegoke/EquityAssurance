EQUITY PASSENGER INSURANCE

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:

This is a group personal accident policy that pays benefits to fee paying passengers of commercial vehicles in return for premium paid. The premium will be a percentage of the transport fare and period of cover is the duration of the trip

Missing goods resulting from accident will not be covered.

2. Death Benefit

The policy pays a total sum of N 500,000 to the estate of the passenger insured who suffers death resulting from motor accident.

3. Permanent Total Disability

The policy pays a total sum of N 500,000 each to the estate of passengers who suffers permanent resulting from motor accident.

4. Emergency Medical Expenses/Burial and Funeral Expenses

The policy pays a total sum of N 50,000 each to the estate of passengers who incurs medical, burial and funeral expenses resulting from motor accident.

PRICING:

PREMIUM PAYABLE N500.00 PER TRIP/DAY
