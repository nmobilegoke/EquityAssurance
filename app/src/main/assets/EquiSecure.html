EQUI SECURE

DESCRIPTIONS:

The product will have a saving component and a protection component


Product Features:

1. Contribution limit Contribution paid will be credited into the saving vehicle and the minimum contribution will be N100.00 per day The policy has no limit on the amount of contribution a policy holder can make

2. Premium frequency Frequency of contribution will be payable either daily, weekly or monthly and in advance

3. Interest rate

The interest credited will be guaranteed at a minimum rate of 3%

4. Death and Permanent Disability Benefit

ยง The policy will pay a death benefit equal to the sum assured (N500,000) should the Life Assured die through an accident ยง The policy pays a permanent disability benefit equal to the sum assured (N500,000) ยง The policy pays a medical expense up to a maximum of N50,000

5. Maturity Benefit The policy pays the account balance on maturity. The account balance comprises of the contribution plus guaranteed interest rate

6. Entry Age Limit The minimum and maximum age at entry will be 18 and 60 years respectively

7. Risk premium Mortality and permanent disability benefit charge will be deducted from contribution to cover for death and permanent disability benefit
